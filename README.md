# About Me

I am a **Computer Engineering Undergraduate at Telkom University** exploring the intersection of **Artificial Intelligence, Cybersecurity, Embedded Systems, and Secure Software Engineering**.

Currently, I contribute as an **AI Research Assistant at Telkom University**, a **Research Assistant Intern at Indonesia’s National Research and Innovation Agency (BRIN)**, and a **Junior Researcher in the Penetration Testing Division of the Security Laboratory**.

My work spans from building AI-powered educational platforms and industrial intelligence systems to researching cybersecurity solutions and embedded technologies.

Rather than rushing to define a single specialization, I intentionally explore multiple engineering disciplines to understand how intelligent systems are designed, deployed, secured, and scaled.

### Current Interests

* Agentic AI Systems
* Large Language Models (LLMs)
* AI-Powered Educational Technology
* Industrial Intelligence Systems
* Penetration Testing
* Vulnerability Assessment
* IoT Security
* Embedded Systems Engineering
* Cloud Infrastructure Automation

### Leadership & Community

Beyond technical development, I am passionate about empowering future technologists through mentoring, student organizations, research communities, and technology education initiatives.

> **"I Don't Know, But I'll Find Out."**

This mindset drives how I approach engineering, research, leadership, and lifelong learning.

### Open To

* AI Research Collaborations
* Research Assistant Opportunities
* Cybersecurity Communities
* Open Source Contributions
* Engineering Internships
* Technology Mentorship Programs

We focus 100% on accelerating members' technical confidence and forging unbreakable personal bonds behind closed doors.

**Scope of Work & Strategic Execution:**
* **Community Engineering:** Engineering a supportive network designed to eliminate isolation. When female engineers face imposter syndrome or academic fatigue in male-dominated lab environments, they receive immediate emotional support and strategic navigation from their tech sisters.
* **Overcoming the Confidence Gap:** Designing a launchpad that empowers members to transition from hiding their code to confidently publishing their GitHub repositories and broadcasting technical storytelling on LinkedIn—stepping into the spotlight to own their brilliant ideas.
* **Democratizing Insider Access:** Leveraging institutional and research networks to bridge the privilege gap, granting qualified members direct opportunities to co-author and get published in research journal papers.
